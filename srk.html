SRK

Shah Rukh Khan (SRK), known as the "King of Bollywood," is one of the most celebrated and influential actors in Indian cinema. Here are some key details about him: - **Full Name**: Shah Rukh Khan - **Born**: November 2, 1965, in New Delhi, India - **Profession**: Actor, Producer, Entrepreneur - **Career Start**: He began his career in the late 1980s with television series like *Fauji* and *Circus* before transitioning to films. - **Debut Film**: *Deewana* (1992), which earned him the Filmfare Award for Best Male Debut. ### Achievements: - **Awards**: Over 300 awards, including 14 Filmfare Awards. - **Iconic Films**: - *Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge* (1995) - *Kabhi Khushi Kabhie Gham* (2001) - *My Name Is Khan* (2010) - *Chennai Express* (2013) - *Pathaan* (2023) - **Production Company**: Co-owner of Red Chillies Entertainment, a major production and visual effects company. - **Entrepreneurship**: Co-owns the Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) IPL cricket team. ### Philanthropy: - SRK is actively involved in numerous humanitarian causes, including child health and education initiatives. He was honored with UNESCO's Pyramide con Marni award for his charitable efforts. ### Global Influence: - **Recognition**: Known worldwide, with fans spanning across continents. - **Titles**: Often referred to as the "Baadshah of Bollywood" or the "Global Superstar." - **TED Talks Speaker**: Delivered a widely acclaimed talk at TED in 2017.
