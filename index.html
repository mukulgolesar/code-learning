Tea Recipe
Ingredients
  • Sugar
  • Tea Powder
  • Bowl
  • Milk
  • Water

Recipe
  1. Take a bowl keep it on a stove pour half cup of water in it and start boilling it
  2. Pour 1 table spoon tea powder and 2 table spoon sugar in boiling water
  3. After perfet boiling of it mix half cup of milk in it and kept it for 2 to 3 min on stove
  4. After that filter it and we get our Tea
